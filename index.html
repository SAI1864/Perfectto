Studio process

A transparent workflow from inspection to handover

Every vehicle moves through a disciplined process designed to clean deeply, correct safely, protect intelligently, and give you clear aftercare instructions.

Inspect

Paint Mapping & Decontamination

Paint, glass, wheels, trim, and interior materials are assessed before a careful wash, iron removal, clay treatment, and surface reset.

Correct

Paint Correction & Refinement

Swirls, haze, oxidation, and light defects are corrected through measured machine polishing for clarity, color depth, and gloss.

Protect

Ceramic Protection & Final Detail

Coating, trim dressing, glass treatment, tire finish, and final inspection create a protected vehicle that leaves the studio ready to be noticed.
